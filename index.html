Kaju Barfi

Ingredients

  • Nestlé MILKMAID
  • Nestlé a+ Toned Milk
  • Kaju (Cashew nuts)
  • Khoya
  • Maida (All Purpose Flour)

Recipe

  1. Crush kaju to a fine powder. Put all the ingredients together and grind to a smooth paste.
  2. Transfer to a thick bottomed kadai and cook on a slow fire until the mixture starts leaving the slides and forms a ball.
  3. Put the mixture in a tray and roll out thinly (1/8”). Cool and cut into diamond shapes.

Instructions

  1. Blend cashews and milk in a blender to a fine paste.
  2. Mix the paste and sugar and cook over low heat, stirring until the sugar dissolves, then bring to a boil.
  3. Continue stirring over medium heat until the mixture leaves the sides of the pan and becomes a dough-like paste.
  4. Remove from heat and when cool enough to handle, roll it onto a greased surface with a greased rolling pin.
  5. Now cover with the silver leaf and leave to cool, then cut into diamond-shaped pieces.
