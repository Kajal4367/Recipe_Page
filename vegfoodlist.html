Allu Gobi Masala

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cauliflower
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Cashews or almond flour or cream
  • Green chili
  • Spices

Recipe

  1. firstly, add oil and saute jeera, bay leaf and cinnamon stick till they turn aromatic.
  2. further, add chili powder and turmeric and saute for 20 seconds on low flame. this helps to give bright red colour to curry.
  3. add prepared onion-tomato paste and saute for 5 minutes.
  4. also add coriander powder and salt. saute till the raw smell of coriander powder disappears.
  5. furthermore, add cubed potatoes and water. mix well.
  6. cover and cook for 10 minutes or till the potatoes are almost cooked.
  7. finally, serve aloo gobi curry with chapathi, roti or rice.

Instructions

  1. Do not add too much oil
  2. Do not add too much salt
  3. Do not use too much of water
  4. Do not use boiled potatoes

Steam Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • water
  • lemon juice
  • Salt

Recipe

  1. Clean the rice and wash it well until the water is clear1.
  2. Soak the rice in 2 cups of water for at least 1/2 hour1.
  3. In a heavy-bottomed pan, put the rice and water over high heat and bring to a boil, adding lemon juice if desired1.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let the rice simmer until it is tender and has absorbed all the water
  5. Turn off the heat and let the rice sit in the pan with the lid on for at least 5 more minutes

Tadka Dal

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • spices
  • water
  • oil or ghee

Recipe

  1. Rinse 1 cup of toor dal or masoor dal for a couple of times in water
  2. Add the lentils to a pressure cooker or pot along with water, turmeric, and salt
  3. Cook the lentils until they are soft and mushy
  4. Add the cooked lentils to the pan with the spices and mix well

Instructions

  1. Do not add too much water
  2. Do not add too much salt
  3. Do not add too much oil or ghee

Paneer Do Pyaza

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • onion
  • water
  • oil
  • spices

Recipe

  1. Add capsicum, tomatoes, and cashews to a blender jar.
  2. Heat a pan with oil. Add jeera (cumin seeds) and cardamoms.
  3. Fry until the onions turn golden and shrink in size

Instructions

  1. Do not add too much water
  2. Do not add too much salt
  3. Do not add too much oil or ghee
