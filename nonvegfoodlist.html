Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Onions
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Coconut milk
  • Tamarind paste
  • oil

Recipe

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Once they start to pop, add cumin seeds and coriander seeds.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and cook until they are golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a few minutes until the raw smell goes away.
  4. Add coconut milk and tamarind paste to the pan and bring to a simmer.
  5. Add fish pieces to the pan and cook until the fish is cooked through.

Instructions

  1. Do not add too much oil
  2. Do not add too much salt
  3. Do not use too much of water

Mutton Kosha

Ingredients

  • Mutton
  • Onions
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Yogurt
  • Oil or ghee

Recipe

  1. Marinate the mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan and add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves.
  3. Once the spices are fragrant, add onions and cook until they are golden brown.
  4. Add the marinated mutton to the pan and cook on high heat until it is browned on all sides.
  5. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, and garam masala to the pan and mix well.
  6. Cook the mutton on low heat until it is tender and the gravy has thickened.

Instructions

  1. Do not add too much oil
  2. Do not add too much salt
  3. Do not use too much of water
